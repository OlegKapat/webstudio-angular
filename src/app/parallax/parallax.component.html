Why you need to evaluate the company's website.

How effective is your website? Is there a good return on business for him? It's amazing how many entrepreneurs can not answer these simple questions. And after the launch of the site itself, months could pass, and it is still not clear how well it contributes to the goals for which it was created, and whether it justifies the investments made. The thing is that the owner can not give an answer to the question about the effectiveness of his site simply because he does not know. And does not know, because no one is engaged in measuring and tracking such things. And for business it's really bad.
